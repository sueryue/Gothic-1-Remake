👥 NPC Character Database

Key characters, their locations, roles, and what they offer

🔥 Old Camp Characters

Old Camp
Diego
Mentor / Main Quest Giver
Location
Beach path (first meeting), then Old Camp
Role
Your guide through the early game. First friendly face in the colony. Teaches Strength & Agility, explains factions and politics, gives main quest progression.
When You Meet
Within the first 10 minutes, on the path from the beach
Trainer (STR, AGI)Quest Giver
Old Camp
Gomez
Old Camp Leader
Location
Castle (Old Camp inner area)
Role
Rules the Old Camp with an iron fist. Controls ore mining operations and trade. Final decision-maker for faction initiation. Represents the old Baron class hierarchy.
When You Meet
Mid-game, during Old Camp initiation quests
Quest Giver
Old Camp
Torres
Fire Mage / Magic Trainer
Location
Old Camp (Fire Mage quarter)
Role
Sells magical items, runes, and mana upgrades. Teaches fire magic — basic and advanced runes. Your go-to for anything magic-related in Old Camp.
When You Meet
Early game, as soon as you arrive at Old Camp
Magic TrainerMerchant
Old Camp
Wesley
Guard Captain
Location
Old Camp (guard quarters)
Role
Guard captain who gives the "Sword of Sentry" quest — your first real weapon upgrade. Provides patrol route information and combat-related quests.
When You Meet
Early game, after arriving at Old Camp
Quest GiverCombat
Old Camp
Skirti
Weapon Trainer
Location
Old Camp (training area)
Role
One-handed weapon specialist. Teaches sword combat techniques and increases one-handed weapon proficiency.
When You Meet
Early game, Old Camp training grounds
Trainer (1H Weapons)
Old Camp
Graham
Cartographer / Merchant
Location
Old Camp (near market area)
Role
Sells maps with routes marked. Essential purchase early game — his maps show you safe paths, danger zones, and point-of-interest locations throughout the colony.
When You Meet
After talking to Wesley, who sends you to Graham
Merchant (Maps)
Old Camp
Chef Snuf
Cook / Early Quest Giver
Location
Old Camp (kitchen area)
Role
Gives the "Cooking Ingredients" quest — one of your first fetch quests. Sells food supplies and buys herbs/meat. Good source of early XP and ore coins.
When You Meet
Very early, as soon as you explore Old Camp
Quest GiverMerchant (Food)

💧 New Camp Characters

New Camp
Lares
New Camp Leader / Quest Giver
Location
New Camp (central area)
Role
Leads the New Camp rebellion. Gives the modified supply list quest and initiation tasks. Coordinates the plan to breach the magical barrier. Your main New Camp contact.
When You Meet
When first visiting New Camp (Phase 2-3)
Quest GiverTrainer
New Camp
Rice Lord
Wealthy Trader / Quest Chain
Location
New Camp (merchant district)
Role
Controls New Camp's supply lines. His quest chain rewards the legendary Rice Lord's Unique Dagger. A key figure for New Camp's economic and diplomatic quests.
When You Meet
Mid-game, New Camp quest progression
Quest ChainMerchant
New Camp
Mordrag
Key NPC / Ring Quest
Location
New Camp / traveling
Role
His ring is a required item for New Camp initiation. Can be acquired through trade, theft, or force — your choice affects reputation. Also provides travel escort services.
When You Meet
During New Camp initiation quests
Quest Item
New Camp
Cavalorn
Hunter / Guide
Location
Cabin along the western road to New Camp
Role
Lives in a cabin between Old Camp and New Camp. Offers hunting tips and warns travelers about dangers on the road. A friendly face on the journey between camps.
When You Meet
When traveling from Old Camp to New Camp
Trainer (Hunting)

🌀 Swamp Camp Characters

Swamp Camp
Y'Berion
Swamp Camp Leader / High Priest
Location
Swamp Camp (temple area)
Role
Spiritual leader of the Swamp Camp. Oversees all initiations and major rituals. Main quest giver for the Swamp Camp path. Communes with the Sleeper.
When You Meet
When visiting Swamp Camp (Phase 2-3)
Quest GiverSpiritual Leader
Swamp Camp
Cor Angar
Templar Leader / Combat Trainer
Location
Swamp Camp (templar quarters)
Role
Leads the Templar order within Swamp Camp. Trains advanced combat techniques for Swamp Camp initiates. Oversees the Templar Trial advancement quest.
When You Meet
Mid-game, after becoming a Novice
Combat TrainerQuest Giver
Swamp Camp
Four Spiritual Guides
Initiation Gatekeepers
Location
Swamp Camp (various temple locations)
Role
Four guides, each demanding different proof to earn their favor: ingenuity, collection, magical capability, and spiritual trial. All four must be impressed to become a Novice. Each one tests a different aspect of your character.
When You Meet
During Swamp Camp initiation
Initiation QuestSpiritual Test

🌐 Other Notable Characters

Neutral
Pinks
Stealth Trainer
Location
Hidden (must be found via quest)
Role
Unlocks stealth skills — sneaking, pickpocketing, and silent movement. Must complete "Find Pinks" quest to access his training. Available to all factions but must be found first.
Trainer (Stealth)
Neutral
Cole & Kyle
Arena Fighters
Location
Old Camp Arena
Role
Two fighters in the Old Camp arena. Challenging them gives safe combat practice (you can't die in arena matches). Good for learning combat timing and earning early XP. Kyle is the tougher of the two.
Combat Practice
Neutral
Aidan
Hunter / Trader
Location
Cabin along New Camp route
Role
Lives in a cabin near New Camp. Buys and sells hunting supplies. Good source for arrows and hunting gear if you're going a ranged build.
Merchant (Hunting)
Neutral
Nick
Missing Guard

The subject of Wesley's "Missing Guard" quest. Finding him reveals patrol route information and teaches you about the dangers outside camp walls. An early investigation quest.
