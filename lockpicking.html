Introduction to Lockpicking

⚠️ Important: Lockpicking is one of the most complex mechanics in Gothic 1 Remake. The puzzle locks require you to press buttons in the correct sequence. Getting it wrong damages the lock!

How Puzzle Locks Work

Puzzle locks consist of multiple "plates" with colored buttons. Your goal is to make all buttons green by pressing them in the correct order.

The Basic Rules:

  1. 🔴 Red: Changes only that button
  2. 🔵 Blue: Changes that button + adjacent
  3. 🟡 Yellow: Changes that button + same row/column

Goal: Get ALL buttons to green. Each wrong attempt damages the lock — too much damage = broken permanently. Always practice in Training Center first!

Collapsed Tower Lock

This is the most complex lock in the game, located at the collapsed tower near the entrance to the Bartered Valley. It features 7 plates with interconnected mechanics.

Basic Training Center Locks (1-4 Plate)

1-Plate Lock

Easy

Simply press the button. If it's red, one press. If blue, may need two presses to cycle back to green.

2-Plate Lock

Easy

Press each button once. If there's interaction between plates, press the one that affects the other first.

3-Plate Lock

Medium

Start from the edges. Press outer plates first, then the center. Watch for blue/yellow interactions.

4-Plate Lock

Medium

Final training lock. Practice all patterns before moving to world locks. Use our calculator for specific configurations.

Pro Tips & Tricks

💾 Save Frequently

Always save before attempting a new lock. Reload if you break it.

🎯 Practice First

Use the Training Center to learn patterns before risking important world locks.

🔧 Keep Repair Kits

Carry lock picks to repair damaged locks. One kit can fix about 3 attempts.

📝 Write It Down

For complex locks, jot down the sequence while memorizing.

🧠 Pattern Recognition

Look for repeating color patterns. Many locks use symmetrical sequences.

Lockpicking FAQ

What happens if I break a lock?

Broken locks cannot be opened until repaired. Use a lock pick tool. If completely broken, reload a save.

Can I skip lockpicking entirely?

Most locks have alternative paths, but they often require more exploration or combat.

Are there cheats for locks?

The game doesn't include a built-in skip. Check Steam Workshop for community mods.

Why do locks get harder as I progress?

Intentional design. Early training locks teach basics, later locks challenge memory and pattern recognition.
