🗺️ Map & Exploration Guide

Navigate the Valley of the Mines safely — travel routes, landmarks, and exploration tips

🗺️

The Valley of the Mines

Three camps separated by dangerous wilderness. The magical barrier surrounds the entire colony. Understanding the layout is key to survival.

📸 Want to contribute a map screenshot? Visit our Community page →

🛤️ Verified Travel Routes

⚠️ Travel Safety: All routes below have been verified from multiple guide sources. Always save before traveling.

Old Camp → New Camp (Main Route)

Difficulty: Easy | Min Level: 4 | Duration: ~5 minutes

From the Old Camp south gate, follow the western road toward the lake. Pass Cavalorn's cabin, then Aidan's cabin. Continue west to the lake where New Camp sits on the hill.

⚠️ Dangers: Wolves (level 3+), occasional bandits (level 4+)

Old Camp → Swamp Camp

Difficulty: Hard | Min Level: 5 | Duration: ~10 minutes

More dangerous route through marshland. Exit Old Camp and head through dense forest, avoiding orc patrol paths. The swamp terrain slows movement — stay on elevated ground.

⚠️ Dangers: Giant Spiders, Shadowbeasts (night), poisonous marsh gas

New Camp → Old Mine

Difficulty: Medium | Min Level: 5 | Duration: ~8 minutes

From New Camp, head northeast along the ridge past the old watchtower. The mine entrance is visible from a distance. Required for Diego's trial.

⚠️ Dangers: Minecrawlers near entrance, patrols near the mining area

New Camp → Collapsed Tower

Difficulty: Extreme | Min Level: 10 | Duration: ~15 minutes

The most dangerous journey. The Collapsed Tower is near the entrance to Bartered Valley. Follow the mountain pass east of New Camp, past orc border outposts. Contains the game's most complex lock (7 plates).

⚠️ Dangers: Orc Warriors, Orc Scouts, Shadowbeasts. Bring repair kits.

🧭 Exploration Tips

🗺️ Buy Maps

Purchase maps from Graham (Old Camp) after talking to Wesley. Maps mark routes, resource nodes, and danger zones. Worth every ore coin.

🏔️ Use High Ground

Climb hills and ridges to scout ahead. You can see enemy patrols, camp locations, and landmarks from elevated positions.

🌅 Travel By Day

Most enemies are more aggressive at night. Shadowbeasts and undead grow stronger in darkness. Plan journeys for daylight hours.

🏃 Know Escape Routes

Before entering any new area, identify at least one escape path. Running into a dead end while being chased is the #1 cause of early deaths.

🏚️ Use Cabins as Checkpoints

Cavalorn's and Aidan's cabins between camps are safe zones. Use them to save, rest, and restock before continuing your journey.

💎 Resource Nodes

Basic materials (herbs, mushrooms, berries) respawn daily at set locations. Mark these on your mental map for consistent income.
