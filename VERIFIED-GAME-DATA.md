### **📖 Story Background (Verified)**

**Setting:** Kingdom of Myrtana - Valley of the Mines

The Kingdom has been invaded by orcs. King Rhobar II needs magical ore to forge weapons, so he forces all prisoners to work in the Khorinis mines. A magical barrier is erected to prevent escape, but something goes wrong - the magic spirals out of control, trapping everyone inside.

The prisoners rebel, creating a lawless territory ruled by brutal inmates. Now the King must negotiate with these new rulers as tensions rise among factions. An unknown prisoner (you) arrives and will change everything.

### **⚔️ Three Factions - Verified Data**

#### **1. Old Camp (Stary Obóz) 🔥**

**Position:** Largest camp, central location near castle  
**Magic:** Fire Mages  
**Best For:** Beginners, players who want structured progression  

**Key Features:**
- Easiest intro quests for new players
- Early access to fire magic (Flame spells, Fire runes)
- High-quality armor available early game
- Well-organized structure with clear hierarchy
- Can trade with merchants easily
- Near training mentors (one-handed weapons, strength/agility)

**NPC Mentors:**
- **Diego** - Teaches Strength & Agility stats
- **Skirti** - One-handed weapon training
- **Torres** - Rune magic, mana upgrades, sells magical items
- **Graham** - Provides maps with routes marked
- **Wesley** - Guard captain, gives "Sword of Sentry" quest
- **Chef Snuf** - Cooking/food supply
- **Guy** - Assigns temporary housing

**Joining Requirements (Verified):**
1. Talk to Diego at start (follow him to Old Camp)
2. Complete minimum 6 main quests including:
   - Missing Guard (find guard Nick)
   - Chef's Worms (3 meat worms, 5 mushrooms)
   - Challenge Two Fighters (defeat Cole/Kyle in arena)
   - Find Pinks (stealth skill teacher)
3. Get level 5+
4. Get support from at least 4 Shadows members
5. Pass Diego's test (retrieve supply list from Old Mine)
6. Meet Gomez in castle and name 4 valid contacts

**Rewards for Joining:**
- Shadow Armor (unique faction armor)
- Large XP boost
- Ore coins as currency
- Access to Inner Circle treasures

#### **2. New Camp (Nowy Obóz) 💧**

**Position:** Western lake area, near hills  
**Magic:** Water Mages (Water Ring can freeze enemies)  
**Best For:** Combat-focused players, high-risk/high-reward seekers  

**Key Features:**
- Criminal-themed storylines
- 2H weapon training available early
- Unique powerful weapons not available elsewhere
- Less hierarchical, more independent society
- Expanded quests in Remake compared to original
- Early challenge but better late-game rewards

**Notable Quests (Verified):**
- **Missing Bran Investigation** - New in Remake, leads to new map areas
- **Food Convoy Ambush** - Attack resource transport between Old Camp & Old Mine
- **Modifying Supply Lists** - Work with Rice Lord
- **Acquiring Mordrag's Ring** - Special artifact quest

**Joining Requirements (Verified):**
1. Reach level 5+
2. Modify and deliver supply list to Lares
3. Acquire Mordrag's ring
4. Gather mineral/resources for various tasks
5. Earn trust of key characters
6. Collaborate with Rice Lord

**Combat Style:**
- Melee focused
- Two-handed weapons
- Bow/archery builds possible
- Higher difficulty early but stronger late-game equipment

#### **3. Swamp Camp (Obóz Bagna) 🌀**

**Position:** Hidden in marshland  
**Magic:** Spirit Magic (Sleep, Telekinesis, Wind Hammer)  
**Best For:** Spell-control players, mystical storyline lovers  

**Key Features:**
- Spiritual focus on Sleeper God
- Collect swamp herbs for sustenance
- Unique spirit magic unavailable in other camps
- Early spell variety (can use special spells from game early)
- Tactical combat playstyle
- Templar-style fighting combined with magic

**Unique Spells (Verified):**
- **Sleep** - Put enemies to sleep
- **Telekinesis** - Move objects/enemies
- **Wind Hammer** - Magical attack spell
- Various control/utility spells unique to this faction

**Joining Requirements (Verified):**
1. Reach level 5+
2. Impress 4 spiritual guides (not military leaders)
3. Each guide requires different test:
   - Demonstrate ingenuity
   - Complete collection tasks  
   - Prove magical capabilities
4. Once all 4 guides favor you, accepted as novice
5. Begin path toward Templar ranks

**Playstyle Notes:**
- Perfect for tactical players
- Spell crowd control specialist
- Different/mystical storytelling approach
- Most unique of three factions

### **🗺️ Travel Routes (Verified)**

**Old Camp → New Camp Route:**
From Old Camp south gate, follow western road toward lake. Landmarks along way:
1. Cavalorn's cabin (first stop)
2. Aidan's cabin (by water, second stop)
3. Continue west to lake area where New Camp sits on hill

**Important Note:** Not recommended for levels 1-3. Level 4-5+ recommended due to wolves, bandits, beasts on path.

### **🔐 Lockpicking System (Verified from Gameplay)**

**Core Mechanic:**
Puzzle locks require pressing buttons in correct sequence to make ALL buttons green. Wrong attempts damage the lock. Too much damage = broken lock.

**Button Types:**
- 🔴 Red Button - Changes only that button
- 🔵 Blue Button - Changes that button + adjacent buttons  
- 🟡 Yellow Button - Changes that button + same row/column

**Difficulty Levels:**
1. **Basic** - 1-4 plates (Training Center practice)
2. **Intermediate** - 5 plate (standard puzzles)
3. **Advanced** - 6-7 plate (harder locks)
4. **Expert** - Collapsed Tower (57 steps, most complex)

**Tip:** Use saves before difficult locks! Manual saves are permanent, quicksave (Q key) is not.

**Source:** Verified from Steam Demo (Nyras Prologue) gameplay reviews

### **⚠️ Important Warnings (Official)**

1. **Faction Choice is Permanent**
   - Once you choose a faction at Camp Checkpoint, cannot switch without restarting
   - Strategy: Complete quests for ALL THREE camps before committing!

2. **Death Penalties**
   - You lose experience when dying
   - Quicksave recommended before fights
   - Force save (manual) is permanent

3. **Early Game Difficulty**
   - First few hours intentionally challenging
   - Level 1-3 is hardest period
   - Don't engage stronger enemies - flee and come back stronger

4. **Resource Management**
   - Berry, herbs, raw meat restore health
   - Sell excess resources for ore coins
   - Basic materials spawn daily at set locations

**Sources:** Steam Demo reviews, multiple Chinese guide sites (9game, ali213, 52pk)

### **💡 Pro Tips Verified from Multiple Sources**

1. **Follow Diego Early** - He protects you from mobs, explains world lore
2. **Gather Everything Early** - White tier resources (berries, herbs, meat) sell for coins
3. **Talk Before Deciding** - Meet guards, leaders from all camps before choosing
4. **Use Training Mentors** - Skirti (weapons), Diego (stats), Torres (magic)
5. **Map Purchase** - Buy from Graham after talking to Wesley for route markers
6. **Save Often** - This is NOT a casual game - expect challenges
7. **Level Plan Before Pointing Skills** - Don't randomly allocate stat points
8. **Quest Priority** - Main storyline > Side quests > Random encounters
