📊 Leveling Strategy

⚠️ Critical Advice: In Gothic, you CANNOT max everything. Each build requires focus. Spreading stats evenly creates a weak character that struggles in the endgame.

1. Plan Early

Decide your build by level 5. Know your primary and secondary stats before you start spending LP. Use the Faction Quiz if unsure.

2. Prioritize One Weapon

Master ONE weapon type — one-handed, two-handed, or magic. Splitting between them wastes LP. Weapon proficiency determines 80% of your damage output.

3. LP Scaling

Each stat point costs more LP as it increases. The first 30 points in a stat are cheap. Points 60-80 are expensive. Plan accordingly.

4. Train Before Fights

Visit trainers after every 2-3 levels. Spending LP immediately makes the next fights easier. Hoarding LP gives no benefit.

5. Secondary Skills

After your primary combat stat hits 60, invest 10-15 points in a secondary. Pure builds do one thing well; hybrids are more versatile.

6. Mana for Non-Casters

Even melee builds benefit from 10-15 Mana for basic utility. Some weapon enchants and rings require minimum mana thresholds.
