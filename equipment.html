⚔️ Equipment Guide

Every weapon, armor set, and piece of gear — organized by faction and rarity

Faction-Specific Equipment

🔥 Old Camp Gear

Premium armor available early game. Fire magic focus. Best heavy armor progression in the game.

  • Rare Shadow Armor (Initiation Reward)
  • Epic Guard Captain's Sword
  • Rare Fire Rune - Basic (from Torres)
  • Epic Inner Circle Armor

💧 New Camp Gear

Unique weapons not found elsewhere. Best two-handed and ranged options. High-risk, high-reward loadout.

  • Rare Mercenary Leather Armor
  • Epic Heavy Two-Handed Axe
  • Rare Water Ring (Freeze enchant)
  • Legendary Rice Lord's Unique Dagger

🌀 Swamp Camp Gear

Light armor with magic bonuses. Unique spells and magical items. Tactical, not tanky.

  • Rare Novice Robes
  • Epic Templar Armor
  • Rare Sleep Spell Scroll
  • Legendary Spirit Staff

💰 How to Get Better Gear

🎁 Quest Rewards

Faction initiation quests give the best gear. Old Camp: Shadow Armor from Diego's trial. New Camp: unique weapons from Rice Lord. Swamp Camp: Templar equipment from spiritual guides.

🛒 Merchants

Torres (Old Camp) sells magical items and runes. New Camp merchants stock unique weapons. Graham sells maps — essential for finding hidden gear caches.

🔐 Locked Chests

The best equipment is behind puzzle locks. Collapsed Tower (7 plates) guards legendary items. Use the Lock Solver to crack them without damage.

⚔️ Enemy Drops

Orc warriors drop high-tier weapons. Shadowbeasts drop rare hides (crafting). Minecrawlers drop chitin (armor crafting). Boss enemies drop legendary-tier loot.

🔨 Crafting

Combine monster drops at crafting stations. Shadow Hide + Ore = reinforced armor. Venom Glands + weapon = poison coating. Spider Silk + leather = light armor upgrade.

🔄 Trading Economy

Sell excess herbs, meat, and monster parts for ore coins. Buy better gear from faction merchants. Prices vary by camp — shop around before committing.
