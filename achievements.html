📚 Complete Achievement Catalog

Gothic 1 Remake features 42 Steam achievements across story progression, combat challenges, exploration milestones, and hidden secrets. The 10 highlighted achievements above are the most notable — the remaining 32 include faction-specific completions, boss kills, skill mastery, and collectible hunting.

💡 Achievement Hunting Tips: Many achievements are faction-specific — you'll need at least 3 playthroughs (one per faction) to unlock everything. Use the Lock Solver for "The Fixer" achievement (solve all locks without breaking lockpicks). For "The Anatomaniac," consult the Bestiary to track every creature.

📎 Achievement Categories

Story Main Story

Achievements tied to main quest progression and faction storylines. Most players will unlock these naturally.

Combat Combat Challenges

Defeat specific enemies, survive difficult encounters, or win fights using specific tactics. Test your combat mastery.

Exploration Exploration

Discover hidden locations, solve puzzles, and uncover secrets across the colony. Rewards thorough exploration.

Collection Collection

Gather all items, trophies, or complete the bestiary. For completionist players who want to see everything.

Skill Skill Mastery

Max out stats, learn all spells, or master specific abilities. Requires dedicated skill grinding and LP management.

Hidden Hidden Secrets

Secret achievements with hidden unlock conditions. Discover them through experimentation, exploration, or community hints.
