🛡️ Old Camp (Knights) - The honorable path of warriors

🌟 Standout Features

  • ✅ Leadership Skills: Command NPC allies in combat
  • ✅ Elite Armor: Access to best physical protection
  • ✅ Dragon Questline: Epic battles against legendary beasts
  • ✅ Civil War Subplot: Secret conflict adds depth

⚔️ New Camp (Bandits) - The gritty path of survivors

🌟 Standout Features

  • ✅ Stealth System: Sneak, backstab, avoid direct fights
  • ✅ Orc Diplomacy: Work with humans AND orcs
  • ✅ Unique Loot: Thieves' tools, poison weapons
  • ✅ Flexible Quests: Multiple solutions always available

💀 Warning

⚠️ Not Beginner-Friendly: New Camp relies heavily on knowing when to run. Many deaths come from overconfidence. Always have an escape plan!

🔮 Sect (Mages) - The intellectual path of arcane mastery

🌟 Standout Features

  • ✅ Spell Repertoire: 10+ unique spells to discover
  • ✅ Summon Ghost: Solve puzzles remotely
  • ✅ Heal Self: Never need medical herbs again
  • ✅ Scholar Status: Special dialogues unlock secret paths
🎯 Important: Sect is ONLY available at game start! You cannot join later without exploits or mods.

❓ Expert FAQ - Advanced Questions Answered

Can I change factions after starting?

Technically yes through the "Wolf Pack" quest line, but it requires strength stat 18+, massive bribes, and has severe narrative consequences. Only recommended for second playthroughs.

Which faction has the BEST loot?

New Camp wins for variety (poisons, thieves' tools, unique daggers). Old Camp has superior armor sets. Sect offers magical items unavailable elsewhere.

Is Sect really too hard for newbies?

Hours 1-5 are brutal, but once you unlock Heal (around hour 6-8), survivability skyrockets. The key is stocking up on mana herbs BEFORE casting. If you struggle, try New Camp first, then Sect on replay.

Which faction is best for speedrunning?

Sect by far. Summon Ghost solves many obstacles instantly. Can skip entire sections of the game. World record runs all use Sect.
