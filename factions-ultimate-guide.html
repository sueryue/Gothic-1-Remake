Expert FAQ

Can I change factions after starting?

Technically yes through the "Wolf Pack" quest line, but it requires strength stat 18+ and has severe narrative consequences. Only recommended for second playthroughs.

Which faction has the BEST loot?

New Camp wins for variety (poisons, thieves' tools). Old Camp has superior armor. Sect offers magical items unavailable elsewhere.

Is Sect really too hard for newbies?

Hours 1-5 are brutal, but once you unlock Heal (around hour 6-8), survivability skyrockets. Stock up on mana herbs before casting.
