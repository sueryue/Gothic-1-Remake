🎬 The Awakening

[Screenshot: beach starting area with magical barrier visible]

1

You Wake Up on the Shore

The game starts with your character unconscious on the beach. Look around to understand the environment. Notice the broken magical barrier in the background — this is the central mystery of the game.

🏕️ Choose Your Faction

Old Camp (Diego)

Fire mages — aggressive combat, heavy armor, clear objectives

New Camp (Lares)

Water mages — stealth, flexibility, multiple approaches

Swamp Camp (Y'Berion)

Spirit magic — unique spells, puzzles, strategic gameplay

⚠️ Point of No Return

Your faction choice is permanent. Complete quests for all three camps before committing!
