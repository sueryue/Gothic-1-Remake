Tips Archive

💾 Save Frequently

Gothic 1 Remake autosaves are sparse. Make manual saves before entering new areas or talking to key NPCs.

🎯 Train Before Fighting

Visit training NPCs in each camp before venturing into dangerous territory. A few extra stat points make all the difference.

🔧 Lockpicking Priority

Learn lockpicking early — many valuable items and shortcuts are locked behind puzzle doors.

⚔️ Faction Timing

Complete quests for all three camps before committing to one. Your faction choice is permanent!

🍖 Stock Food

Food items are your primary healing source early game. Buy from cooks in each camp.

📝 Read Everything

Books and notes often contain lock combinations, hidden quest clues, and lore that unlocks dialogue options.
