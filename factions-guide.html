⚔️ Ultimate Faction Guide

Old Camp, New Camp, or Swamp Camp — what to expect from each faction

⚡ TL;DR — Quick Answer

🎮 First Time Playing? → Choose Old Camp 🔥 (best for beginners, clear objectives, premium armor)

💪 Want Maximum Damage? → Choose New Camp 💧 (water magic late-game power, unique weapon trees)

🔮 Love Arcane Magic? → Choose Swamp Camp 🌀 (early spirit magic, unique spells no one else gets)

Faction Overview

Your faction pick changes everything: which quests you get, what magic you can learn, what armor you wear, and who your allies are. You'll make this choice in the first couple hours of the game.

⚠️ IRREVERSIBLE DECISION: Once you join a faction, you CANNOT switch to another without extreme difficulty. Most players commit to a single playthrough per faction.
🔥

Old Camp (Stary Obóz)

"Order Through Strength"

The largest and most organized faction controlling the main mine and trade routes. Former soldiers and knights value hierarchy, honor, and martial discipline. Home to the Fire Mages and access to premium armor.

💧

New Camp (Nowy Obóz)

"Freedom Through Force"

A rebellious collective of outcasts defying the Baron's rule. They reject hierarchy, valuing individual merit and unconventional tactics. Secretly stockpiling ore to build a device that can tear through the magical barrier.

🌀

Swamp Camp (Obóz Bagna)

"Enlightenment Through Surrender"

A secluded cult in the marshlands worshiping the Dormer God. They've abandoned material wealth for spiritual meditation, using swamp herbs to achieve trance states.

🔥 Old Camp: The Fire Mage Route

Location: Castle ruins in northern valley, controlling main mine and trade routes

Leader: Gomez (representing the Baron class) + Captain Diego (military force)

Pros

  • ✅ Most straightforward for beginners
  • ✅ Strong early-game combat capabilities
  • ✅ Clear quest objectives
  • ✅ Access to powerful armor

Cons

  • ❌ Less stealth options
  • ❌ Linear quest progression
  • ❌ Must confront dragons (very challenging)

Warrior Tank (Early Game)

Strength: 15+Main stat for melee damage
Stamina: 12+Survivability in fights
Combat Skill: SwordsBest damage output

💧 New Camp (Bandits) Route

Location: West from the starting valley toward orc territory

Pros

  • ✅ Flexible playstyle (stealth or brute force)
  • ✅ More freedom in quest approaches
  • ✅ Access to thieves' tools and stealth abilities

Cons

  • ❌ Lower individual combat power initially
  • ❌ Often overwhelmed by numbers
  • ❌ Less honorable allies (some betray you)

Shadow Fighter (Early Game)

Agility: 13+Improved hit-and-run tactics
Strength: 10+Bare minimum for weapon use
Skills: Daggers + ShortbowClose and ranged versatility

🌀 Swamp Camp (Sect) Route

Note: Swamp Camp is ONLY available at game start. You cannot join later without mods or glitches.

Pros

  • ✅ Access to powerful unique spells
  • ✅ Magic trumps many physical challenges
  • ✅ Can heal yourself (regenerate HP)

Cons

  • ❌ Steepest learning curve
  • ❌ Mana management crucial
  • ❌ Weak in close combat initially

First 10 Hours Survival Tips

💰 Level 1-5: Survive

  • Focus on main faction quest
  • Don't wander far alone
  • Collect food and water constantly
  • Avoid high-level creatures
  • Save frequently!

⚔️ Level 5-10: Establish

  • Unlock faction-specific abilities
  • Get better weapon and armor
  • Make allies in your faction
  • Explore nearby dungeons cautiously

🎯 Level 10-15: Commit

  • Pursue major faction storyline
  • Level up core stats significantly
  • Prepare for mid-game challenges
  • Consider companion relationships

Faction FAQ

Can I switch factions later?

Technically yes, but it's EXTREMELY difficult. Not recommended for newcomers.

Which faction has the best loot?

New Camp offers more variety (thieves' tools, poison weapons), Old Camp has superior armor. Swamp Camp has unique magical items.

Is Swamp Camp really the hardest?

Yes and no. Early game is VERY hard due to mana management. Once you unlock key spells, it becomes easier.
