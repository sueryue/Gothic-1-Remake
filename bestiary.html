⚔️ Combat Survival Guide

⚠️ The Golden Rule: In Gothic 1 Remake, not every enemy is meant to be fought when you first meet them. Knowing when to run is the most important survival skill.

Lv 1-3

Flee everything. Even wolves are deadly. Collect herbs and avoid combat entirely.

Lv 4-6

Fight scavengers, young wolves, and lone molerats. Still run from packs and larger creatures.

Lv 7-10

Engage most wildlife. Shadowbeasts and orcs are still dangerous. Use terrain to your advantage.

Lv 10+

You can handle most enemies. Large groups still require caution. Boss-tier enemies need preparation.

🎯 Combat Strategy Tips

🔍 Scout First

Always scan the area before engaging. Look for additional enemies hiding nearby. A "lone wolf" is rarely alone.

🏃 Know When to Run

If an enemy's health bar barely moves after 3 hits, you're not ready. Run, level up, come back. No shame in tactical retreat.

🎯 Focus Fire

Against groups, pick one target and eliminate it. Weakening multiple enemies but killing none leaves you fighting a full-strength pack.

🛡️ Block vs Dodge

Block small, fast attacks. Dodge heavy, telegraphed attacks. Learning enemy attack patterns is more important than raw stats.

🧪 Use Consumables

Health potions and buffs aren't collectibles — use them. The game gives you resources expecting you to spend them.

🌙 Day vs Night

Some enemies are more active at night. Shadowbeasts and undead grow stronger in darkness. Travel during daylight when possible.
